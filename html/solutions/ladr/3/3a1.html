Exercise Solutions for Exercise 3A.1

Solution:

The solution to the equation \( ax^2 + bx + c = 0 \) can be found using the quadratic formula:

\[ x = \frac{{-b \pm \sqrt{{b^2 - 4ac}}}}{{2a}} \]

Where:

  • \( a \) = coefficient of \( x^2 \)
  • \( b \) = coefficient of \( x \)
  • \( c \) = constant term
